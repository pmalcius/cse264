Jerry the Mouse

Bio

Early Life

Jerry, the pint-sized powerhouse of mischief. Born in a mouse hole that doubled as a cozy hideout, Jerry wasted no time in perfecting the art of cheese pilfering and evading the cat-mania that surrounded him.

The Rival: Tom

The epic rivalry between Jerry and Tom is the stuff of legends. While Tom concocts elaborate schemes involving anvils and mousetraps, Jerry effortlessly dances through the chaos with a smirk that says, "Nice try, whiskers!"

Life at Lehigh

Jerry at Lehigh would turn lectures into comedy shows, transforming textbooks into maze blueprints and doodling elaborate cat-avoidance strategies. His student ID would sport a mischievous grin, and his dorm room antics would make him the legend of campus mischief. Forget straight A's; Jerry would graduate with a degree in hilarity.
